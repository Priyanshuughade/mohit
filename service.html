Our Services

WE ARE A FULL-SERVICE DESIGN / BUILD STUDIO SPECIALIZING IN URBAN GARDENS & OUTDOOR SPACES FOR RESIDENTIAL & COMMERCIAL CLIENTS

Garden Maintainance

Plant Care : From delicate blooms to towering trees, we nurture every plant in your garden with care. Our services encompass pruning, trimming, and disease control to promote healthy growth and vibrant colors.

Weed Control : Weeding is essential for preserving the beauty and health of your garden. Our skilled technicians remove weeds by hand or through eco-friendly methods, ensuring your garden remains pristine.

Soil Management : Healthy soil is the foundation of a thriving garden. We analyze and amend soil composition as needed, providing essential nutrients and promoting optimal plant growth.

Irrigation System Maintenance : Proper irrigation is crucial for the well-being of your plants. We maintain and repair your irrigation system to ensure consistent and efficient water distribution, saving you time and resources.
